Developer update - May 4th 2020

I am currently working on optimizing the game, mainly focusing on performance at the moment. I've had some problems with hitching when removing or placing blocks and that's what I am trying to adress at the moment.

Finally, I updated the website and added this sort of "flow" layout, I hope it's easy to understand and to use. I also added a rad background :)
